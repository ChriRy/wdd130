In my spare time, I like to work on composing and arranging music. I bounce around a lot in regards to genre; I've done musical theater, music from game and movie soundtracks, simplified pop songs for my piano students, and some joke music (which is one of my personal favorites.). I have a long list of ideas for future composing/arranging projects that I plan to work on in the future, but I'm always open to new ideas, so if there is a song you would like me to consider, send me a message.

Learning to compose and arrange has been a long and not always enjoyable process. It's taken a lot of years of piano lessons and trial and error for me to get to where I am today (and honestly, I still have a long way to go). Playing music is tricky, but putting together that tricky-to-play music is another thing altogether. So if you want to learn to compose for yourself and you don't know where to start, send me your questions and concerns and I will try to help as much as I can. Or if you are already working on a piece and just want a second opinion, I can give it a look and give some constructive criticism and advice. Music takes a lot of trial and error (and I've experienced it), but it's always worth it in the end. So keep working on those projects and let me know how I can help.
