Hello! My name is Ryan Christensen. I'm currently working towards a degree in computer science. I'm pretty new to coding and I've really liked it so far.

Some of my hobbies include crocheting, reading, listening to audiobooks, and playing piano. I've been playing piano for about 12 years now and I love it! I'll include a link to some of the music I've arranged.

I've been a member of the church basically my whole life and I love the peace and comfort that comes from knowing that I can always find comfort in Jesus Christ and His gospel, even if everything else is crazy. My favorite scripture is Mosiah 26:30, because I love how no matter what happens or what we do, the Savior will always forgive us.
