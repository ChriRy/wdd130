Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to help my business grow and thrive. With this website, I can post information on the rafting services we provide. People who have used our services can share their experiences and newcomers will be able to see the fun experiences that we have to offer. As more customers come through the website, we will earn more revenue and expand our business to offer even more services.

Audience

My target customers are people who want to add an adventurous experience to their lives. Whether they are a family on vacation looking to make some memories, a group of friends who need a fun activity, or even just a person who is looking for a break from the monotony of everyday life, we can give them the breathtaking, exciting experience they are looking for. We will reach people through our website, mainly designed for laptop and desktop, and share it through social media sites to help spread the word.
